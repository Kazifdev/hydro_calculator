Hydro Power Calculator

Note: How to use the calculator: This calculator allows users to estimate the potential power generation of a hydroelectric system based on parameters such as water flow rate, head (vertical distance the water falls), and turbine efficiency. Users can input these parameters and get an estimation of power output in kilowatts.
